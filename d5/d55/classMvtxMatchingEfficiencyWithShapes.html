◆ calc_dedx()

float MvtxMatchingEfficiencyWithShapes::calc_dedx ( TrackSeed * tpcseed)

Calculate the truncated mean dEdx for a given TPC track seed.

The method extracts all TPC clusters associated with the supplied track seed, applies thickness, geometric alpha and beta corrections, and builds a list of corrected ADC values. The list is sorted and a truncated mean (up to 70 % of the lowest values) is computed and returned.

Parameters
tpcseedPointer to a TrackSeed object containing TPC cluster keys.
Returns
Truncated mean dEdx value after applying corrections.
