Stores matching information between an embedded truth track and a reconstructed track.

Initializes the truth track identifier, the number of truth clusters, and creates a single match entry containing the reconstructed track identifier, associated TPC and SVTX seed identifiers, the number of reconstructed clusters, and the number of matched clusters.

Parameters
id_truthIdentifier of the truth track (default maximum unsigned short).
id_recoIdentifier of the matched reconstructed track (default maximum unsigned short).
nclustruthNumber of clusters belonging to the truth track (default maximum unsigned short).
nclusrecoNumber of clusters belonging to the reconstructed track (default maximum unsigned short).
nclusmatchedNumber of clusters shared between truth and reconstructed tracks (default maximum unsigned short).
id_tpctrackseedIdentifier of the associated TPC track seed (default maximum unsigned short).
id_svtxtrackseedIdentifier of the associated SVTX track seed (default maximum unsigned short).
