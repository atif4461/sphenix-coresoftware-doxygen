int JetKinematicCheck::End ( PHCompositeNode * topNode)
overridevirtual

Called at the end of all processing.

Finalize jet kinematic histograms at the end of processing.

Configures visual style and titles for all jet histograms (pt spectra, eta phi, mass vs pt, pt vs eta, pt vs phi) for each jet radius R02 R03 R04 R05, creates profile histograms for average mass versus pt and eta where needed, and registers all histograms with the histogram manager.
