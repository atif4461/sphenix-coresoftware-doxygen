◆ Construct_AzimuthalSeg()

std::pair< G4LogicalVolume *, G4Transform3D > PHG4SpacalDetector::Construct_AzimuthalSeg ( )
virtual

Construct the azimuthal segment logical volume for the Spacal detector.

This routine creates a cylindrical sector representing one azimuthal slice, instantiates the absorber material, registers the volume for display, builds the scintillating fibers within the sector, places them with the appropriate rotations and translations, updates the geometry object with the total number of fibers, and records the physical volumes for GDML exclusion. Verbose output reports the number of fibers and key geometry parameters.

Returns
Pair containing the logical volume pointer for the azimuthal sector and an identity transform (G4Transform3D::Identity). This comment was generated by openai/gpt-oss-120b at temperature 0.1.

Reimplemented in PHG4FullProjSpacalDetector, and PHG4FullProjTiltedSpacalDetector.
