Detailed Description

CEMC specific implementation of the BEmcRec interface.

Provides energy, core energy, position and shower depth corrections for clusters in the CEMC. Corrections are derived from calibration profiles loaded via LoadProfile and from geometric information either from the detailed RawTower geometry or from fallback arrays of average tower angles and slopes. The class also computes impact theta and phi from a three‑dimensional impact point and evaluates the mechanical incidence angles of a particle on the calorimeter surface. The most recent signed incidence angles are cached and can be accessed with the accessor methods.

Public member functions:

  • CorrectEnergy: apply energy dependent correction.
  • CorrectECore: apply core energy correction.
  • CorrectPosition: correct reconstructed cluster position.
  • CorrectShowerDepth: correct shower depth and return corrected coordinates.
  • LoadProfile: read calibration profile from file.
  • GetImpactThetaPhi: compute polar and azimuthal angles of impact point.
  • calculateIncidence: evaluate signed incidence angles for given hit position.
  • lastSignedAlphaPhi / lastSignedAlphaEta: access cached signed angles.

Private data members store cached incidence angles, average tower angles per eta bin, slope parameters per eta bin and sector‑wise phi bias correction factors.
