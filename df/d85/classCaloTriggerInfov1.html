Detailed Description

CaloTriggerInfov1 provides concrete implementation of CaloTriggerInfo for version 1 It stores best energy and position for various calorimeter patch sizes Methods allow setting and retrieving energy eta and phi for EMCal patches and full calorimeter windows Reset and validity methods are overridden as required by the framework
