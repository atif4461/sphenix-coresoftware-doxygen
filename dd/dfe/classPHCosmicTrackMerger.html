Detailed Description

Merges cosmic track seeds from silicon and tpc detectors.

The module operates within the Fun4All framework as a SubsysReco It retrieves geometry cluster and seed containers from the node tree It can apply zero field handling and dca cuts and iterate merging It can optionally remove outliers

Parameters

m_dcaxycutMaximum transverse distance of closest approach in cm
m_dcarzcutMaximum longitudinal distance of closest approach in cm
m_zeroFieldFlag to treat magnetic field as zero
