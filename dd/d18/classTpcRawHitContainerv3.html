Detailed Description

TpcRawHitContainerv3 implements version3 of TpcRawHitContainer providing storage and access of raw TPC hits It manages a TClonesArray of TpcRawHit objects and tracks event bco and status Functions include Reset identify isValid AddHit overloads get_nhits get_hit and getters setters for status and bco
