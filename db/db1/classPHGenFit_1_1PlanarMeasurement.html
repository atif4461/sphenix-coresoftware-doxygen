Construct a planar measurement from a position, a normal vector and uncertainties.

Parameters
posPosition vector of the measurement.
nNormal vector defining the plane orientation; primarily used when the normal lies in the xy plane (nz equals zero).
duUncertainty along the u direction (orthogonal to the normal within the xy plane).
dvUncertainty along the v direction (cross product of the normal and u, typically along the z axis).

The constructor creates orthogonal basis vectors u and v from the supplied normal. The u vector is obtained via TVector3::Orthogonal() which lies in the xy plane for a normal in that plane. The v vector is the cross product n cross u. These basis vectors together with the uncertainties are passed to init().

If z is not 0, but z <= x or y, then u will still be in xy plane, phi direction error, but v will not be along z axis.
