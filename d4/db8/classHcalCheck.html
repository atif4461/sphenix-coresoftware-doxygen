Detailed Description

HcalCheck analysis module for HCAL raw hit validation. Inherits from SubsysReco to integrate with Fun4All event loop and from DumpPacket for packet dumping utilities. Implements Init to acquire required nodes and process_event to iterate over HCAL hit containers.
