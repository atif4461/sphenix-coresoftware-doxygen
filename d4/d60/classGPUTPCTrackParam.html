◆ CheckNumericalQuality()

bool GPUTPCTrackParam::CheckNumericalQuality ( ) const

Verify that track parameters and covariance matrix contain finite and physically reasonable values.

This routine checks that the position, direction cosine, chi‑square, and all covariance matrix elements are finite. It also enforces positivity of diagonal covariance entries, limits on their magnitudes, and consistency of off‑diagonal terms with the Cauchy‑Schwarz inequality. Additional constraints on the sine of the azimuthal angle and the inverse transverse momentum are applied.

Returns
true if all checks pass, false otherwise. This comment was generated by openai/gpt-oss-120b at temperature 0.1.
