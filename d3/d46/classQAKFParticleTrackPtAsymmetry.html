Detailed Description

QA tool for studying transverse momentum asymmetry of daughter tracks in KFParticle decays.

The class books histograms and fills them with the pt asymmetry defined as (pt1-pt2)/(pt1+pt2) for the two daughter tracks of a reconstructed mother particle Histograms are created for the inclusive sample and differential in mother pseudorapidity and azimuthal angle bins

Parameters
histo_prefixPrefix used for histogram names
min_mMinimum invariant mass of the mother candidate default 0.4 GeV c2
max_mMaximum invariant mass of the mother candidate default 0.6 GeV c2
mother_eta_binsBinning in mother pseudorapidity for differential histograms
mother_phi_binsBinning in mother azimuthal angle for differential histograms

Public interface

  • bookHistograms registers the histograms with a Fun4AllHistoManager
  • analyzeTrackPtAsymmetry loops over tracks in a SvtxTrackMap and fills the asymmetry histograms for a given KFParticle mother
  • setVerbosity controls the amount of debug output This comment was generated by openai/gpt-oss-120b at temperature 0.1.
