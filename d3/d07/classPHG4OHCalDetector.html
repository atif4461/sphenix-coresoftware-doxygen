Detailed Description

PHG4OHCalDetector implements the OHCal detector geometry and field setup within the Fun4All framework It derives from PHG4Detector and provides methods to construct the detector volume and to print detector information The class manages geometry nodes GDML configuration raw tower geometry and assembly volumes for steel absorber and scintillator tiles Detector parameters such as inner radius outer radius length and material volumes are stored and can be queried Super detector name and layer index are configurable through accessor methods Volume accessors allow identification of OHCal volumes and extraction of row column and tower identifiers ConsistencyCheck validates internal geometry mappings and GetRowColumnId returns the hierarchical identifiers for a given physical volume
