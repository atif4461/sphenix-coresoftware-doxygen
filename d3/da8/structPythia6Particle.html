Representation of a particle generated by Pythia6.

Stores status code, PDG identifier, hierarchy indices and full kinematic information including momentum components, energy, mass and vertex coordinates. Also records production time and proper lifetime for use in downstream reconstruction and analysis within the sPHENIX framework.
