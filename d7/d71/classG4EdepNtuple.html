◆ process_event()

int G4EdepNtuple::process_event ( PHCompositeNode * topNode)
overridevirtual

event processing method

Process a single event, extract energy deposition per detector layer and fill the ntuple.

The method iterates over all configured detector node postfixes, retrieves the corresponding PHG4HitContainer from the supplied top node, accumulates the deposited energy (edep) for each layer, and stores the results in the ntuple. In addition to per‑layer entries, a total energy deposition entry (layer index –1) is written for each detector.
