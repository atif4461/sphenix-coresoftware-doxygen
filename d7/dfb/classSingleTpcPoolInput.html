void SingleTpcPoolInput::CleanupUsedPackets ( const uint64_t bclk)
overridevirtual

Remove and delete raw hit packets whose beam clock is less than or equal to the specified value.

Parameters
bclkBeam clock threshold. All entries with a beam clock less than or equal to this value are deleted.

The method iterates over the internal map m_TpcRawHitMap, deletes each packet pointer, and then erases the corresponding keys from m_BclkStack, m_BeamClockFEE and m_TpcRawHitMap. Verbose output is printed when the module verbosity level is greater than 2. This comment was generated by openai/gpt-oss-120b at temperature 0.1.
