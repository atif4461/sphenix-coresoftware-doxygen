DCAPair TrackAnalysisUtils::get_dca ( SvtxTrack * track,
Acts::Vector3 & vertex )

Calculate the distance of closest approach (DCA) of a track to a given vertex.

Parameters
trackPointer to the SvtxTrack object whose DCA is to be computed. Must not be null.
vertexReference to the Acts::Vector3 representing the vertex position.
Returns
A TrackAnalysisUtils::DCAPair containing two pairs:
  • first pair: DCA in the transverse plane (xy) and its uncertainty,
  • second pair: DCA along the beam direction (z) and its uncertainty.

The function transforms the track position and covariance into the local frame defined by the track momentum, extracts the DCA components, and propagates the errors using the rotated covariance matrix. This comment was generated by openai/gpt-oss-120b at temperature 0.1.

First pair is DCA_xy +/- DCA_xy_err

Second pair is DCA_z +/- DCA_z_err
