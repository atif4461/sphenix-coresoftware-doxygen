Detailed Description

Implements stepping action for the TPC detector.

Records energy loss and creates G4 hits for active and absorber volumes

Parameters
detectorPointer to the PHG4TpcDetector instance
parametersPointer to the PHParameters container
Note
Caches frequently used parameters to avoid map lookups during stepping
