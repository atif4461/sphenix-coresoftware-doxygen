Detailed Description

TowerInfoSimv1 provides simulated tower information extending TowerInfov2 Implements Reset Clear copy tower and energy deposition maps for hits and showers Stores hit level energy map in hitedeps and shower level energy map in showeredeps Allows adding hit energy with add edep and shower energy with add shower edep
