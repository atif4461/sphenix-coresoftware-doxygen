Detailed Description

D0 meson particle generator for Geant4 simulation Configurable kinematic ranges for eta rapidity momentum pt and vertex z Provides setters for eta range rapidity range momentum range pt range vertex z range and particle mass Uses internal TF1 functions for sampling distributions Inherits from PHG4ParticleGeneratorBase and implements InitRun and process event
