Detailed Description

The 1-st level of the EMCal clustering: cluster is a set of contiguous towers.

Only used internally by clustering routines.

Compute the 3x3 tower energy sum around the cluster center of gravity.

This method determines the cluster’s center of gravity using the stored hit list, converts the centroid coordinates to integer tower indices, and then delegates to the indexed overload to obtain the summed energy of the nine towers surrounding the central tower.
